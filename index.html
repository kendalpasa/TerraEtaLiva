About TerraEtaLiva

TerraEtaLiva is an application that allows users to explore high-resolution images from NASA's collection. With the help of OpenSeadragon, users can zoom in and out of these images to see incredible details that are not visible to the naked eye. Whether you're a space enthusiast or just curious about the universe, TerraEtaLiva provides an engaging way to discover the wonders of space.
